# Business Insights

## Insight 1 — Overall Funnel Conversion

**Business Question:** How effectively does the customer funnel convert visitors into purchasers?

**Finding:** Out of **120,000 customer sessions**, **8,181 purchases** were completed, resulting in an overall **conversion rate of 6.82%**.

**Recommendation:** Improve conversion by optimizing high drop-off stages in the customer journey, particularly between product views and cart additions.

---

## Insight 2 — Funnel Drop-Off Analysis

**Business Question:** Where do customers leave the purchasing journey?

**Finding:** While **64.89%** of visitors viewed a product, only **34.87%** of those users added an item to the cart. Conversion improved further down the funnel with **59.78%** progressing to checkout and **50.39%** completing their purchase.

**Recommendation:** Focus on increasing the Add-to-Cart rate through improved product pages, clearer pricing, stronger product descriptions and persuasive calls-to-action.

---

## Insight 3 — Device Performance

**Business Question:** Which device performs best?

**Finding:** **Desktop users** achieved the highest conversion rate (**6.91%**), while **Mobile users** generated significantly higher overall revenue (**$11.84M**) because of much larger traffic volume.

**Recommendation:** Continue optimizing the mobile shopping experience to improve conversion while maintaining desktop performance.

---

## Insight 4 — Marketing Channel Performance

**Business Question:** Which acquisition channel drives the strongest business performance?

**Finding:** **Paid Ads** generated the highest revenue (**$7.54M**) and the largest traffic volume, while **Email Marketing** delivered the highest conversion rate (**7.31%**).

**Recommendation:** Continue investing in Paid Ads for customer acquisition while expanding Email Marketing campaigns to maximize conversion efficiency.

---

## Insight 5 — Regional Performance

**Business Question:** Which regions generate the most business value?

**Finding:** The **Metro region** generated **$10.26M** in revenue compared to **$6.76M** in Non-Metro regions, while both regions maintained an identical **6.82% conversion rate**.

**Recommendation:** Increase marketing investment within Metro regions while exploring opportunities to expand revenue in Non-Metro markets.

---

## Insight 6 — New vs Returning Customers

**Business Question:** How do new and returning customers differ?

**Finding:** **New customers** converted slightly better (**6.92%**) than **Returning customers (6.62%)** and generated significantly higher revenue (**$11.23M**).

**Recommendation:** Continue acquiring new customers while strengthening customer retention strategies to increase repeat purchases.

---

## Insight 7 — Discount Effectiveness

**Business Question:** Do discounts improve purchasing behaviour?

**Finding:** Sessions with discounts generated **$8.83M** in revenue and resulted in **4,462 purchases**, outperforming sessions without discounts despite representing a much smaller share of total sessions.

**Recommendation:** Continue using targeted discount campaigns while carefully monitoring profitability to ensure discounts increase both revenue and customer conversion.

---

## Insight 8 — Campaign Performance

**Business Question:** Which marketing campaign performs best?

**Finding:** **Discount campaigns** generated the highest revenue (**$8.53M**), while both **Discount** and **Influencer** campaigns achieved the highest conversion rate (**6.83%**).

**Recommendation:** Continue investing in Discount campaigns for revenue generation while evaluating Influencer campaigns as a cost-effective channel for customer acquisition.
